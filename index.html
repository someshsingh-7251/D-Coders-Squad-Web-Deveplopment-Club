👨‍💻 D-Coder Squad

D-Coder Squad was established on 10th October 2024 under the guidance of Dr. Deepak Painuli (HOD-CSE, COER University, Roorkee). It is the official coding club of the university, created with the aim of enhancing the technical and creative skills of students.

The club comprises several specialized domains, including:

  • 🌐 Web Development
  • 📱 App Development
  • 🎨 Graphics Design
  • 🔬 Research and Development
  • 💻 Competitive Programming
  • 🤖 Machine Learning

The D-Coder Squad provides a platform for students to innovate, collaborate, and grow in the field of technology, preparing them for real-world challenges and opportunities.
